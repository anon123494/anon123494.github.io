 in the name of women , in the name of life

we shall be freed from the chains of servitude

our dark night shall finally turn to dawn

all whips shall be broken

so that our shouts become one of young seeds

so that me ,you and others  become "us" once again

on the pure blood of roses

for this revolution of tears of kisses

in the passing of this unbearable pain

call me my homeland; my life and body

so that the roar of your blood-red call

shakes the world from this uprising


 in the name of women , in the name of life

our cloth of slavery shall be torn

our dark night shall finally turn to dawn

all whips shall be broken

so that our shouts become one of young seeds

so that me ,you and others  become "us" once again


arise for women,life and freedom
